  • Height: 39 in. (91 cm.)

  • Description: The standards shade between beet root and imperial purple on a white ground at base. The falls have a white ground with combination of beet root and imperial purple markings 3/4 of an inch around edge and thin stripe down center. The beard is white at the base, tipped with yellow.
