  • Height: 11in (28cm)
  • Season: E-L
  • Reblooms: reliable September to November
  • Description: This dark-red, plum-violet self little beauty was among the first of the bearded irises to appear at Sans Souci in the spring of 2006.

    Dark irises are difficult to photograph as they tend to look redder and brighter in the photograph than they appear to the human eye. The photo below, taken during in April of 2006, illustrates some of the depth of color in Plum Wine.
