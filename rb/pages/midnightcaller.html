  • Height: 37in (94cm)

  • Reblooms: August through October
  • Description: With its silky, deep-purple iridescent standards and velvety, blackish edged, deeply colored purple falls, and a blue-purple beard, this dark beauty is a standout in the iris garden. It is an excellent grower with a reliable August through October rebloom. When brought inside and displayed under artificial light the intense depth of color in the falls creates an illusion of "negative purple space".
