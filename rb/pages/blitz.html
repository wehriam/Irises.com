Reblooming Standard Dwarf Bearded Iris - BLITZ - Weiler, 1998
Back to Reblooming Iris Master Page
  • Height:10in. (25cm.)
  • Season: E-L
  • Reblooms: September through October.
  • Description: A striking, brilliant yellow, self iris with a yellow beard, Blitz is fragrant, floriferous and an excellent grower.

    At Sans Souci Blitz reblooms reliably late in September/October and on one occasion it rebloomed the second week in December during a mild winter.
