Navy Blue Gem

a cultivar of Iris cristata - Crested Iris - a North American Species

NAVY BLUE GEM
Hybridizer:  Marty Schafer/Jan Sacks
Registered:  1998
Type:  SPEC (cristata)
Height:  4 in. (10 cm)
Bloom Season:  At Sans Souci we see these blooming among the earliest of our Irises.
Description:  Dark blue violet with a bright white signal bordered by very dark blue. There is a light yellow central crest while the two side crests are white. The parentage is unknown but it was in commerce prior to 1985 as "Dark Blue Violet" (Laurie's Garden) and in 1997 as "Navy Blue" (Joe Pye Weed's Garden).
