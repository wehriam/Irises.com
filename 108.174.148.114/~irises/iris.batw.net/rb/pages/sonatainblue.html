- Sonata in Blue Smith, Raymond , 1991
Back to TB Master Page
  • Height: 31in (78cm)
  • Season: M
  • Reblooms: October through November
  • Description: Sonata in blue is a ruffled, brilliant violet-blue with a yellow beard tipped in white. It has a slight fragrance and is an excellent grower but not a reliable rebloomer in this area (Maryland) as it blooms late in October through November and is often nipped by frost. It probably would do better further to the south.
