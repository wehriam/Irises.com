- PINK ATTRACTION (E. Hall, R. 1987)a tall bearded reblooming iris
Back to TB Master Page
  • Height: 30 in. (76 cm.)
  • Season: Early to Mid with a rebloom in Aughust through October
  • Reblooms: yes
  • Description: Pink Attraction is a flared and ruffled iris. The standards and falls are a light pink while the style arms blush with a darker pink. The beards are orange with a light violet streak. The buds show a hint of pale green along the outer mid section of the falls. It can be a strong rebloomer offering "subtle fall" colors to a garden.
