- Istanbul Tall Bearded Iris, M. Byers,1989
Back to TB Master Page
  • Height: 34 in. (86 cm.)
  • Season: Mid season with a rebloom.
  • Description: Istanbul is a ruffled reblooming tall bearded iris with a spicy/musky fragrance. The standards are a clear tuscan yellow while the falls are warm white with a yellow glow at hafts, overlaid with deep bright red purple stripes over upper third which then soften considerably, culminating in narrow pale reddish stitched edges with hint of tan. The beard is indian yellow.
