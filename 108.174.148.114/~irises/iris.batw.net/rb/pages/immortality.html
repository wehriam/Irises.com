- Immortality Zurbrigg , 1972
Back to TB Master Page
  • Height: 30in (76cm)
  • Season: M
  • Reblooms: May through November
  • Description: Immortality is a tall bearded, ruffled, self-white iris with a white beard and a slight, sweet fragrance. Its buds and flowers are pale lavender on cloudy days.

    This reblooming iris is floriferous and a good grower. It usually reblooms in September through October but can blooom from May until the end of October in a good reblooming year.
