Vein Mountain

a cultivar of Iris cristata - Crested Iris - a North American Species



VEIN MOUNTAIN
Hybridizer:  R. Duval
Registered:  1985
Type:  I. cristata
Height:  4-5 in. (10-12 cm)
Bloom Season: Early, At Sans Souci we see these blooming among the earliest of our Irises.
Description:  The standards are pale blue with falls also pale blue with a prominent deep purple line outlining orange crests. The origin is unknown, it was received from T. Kusanagi of Japan as an I. gracilipes.
