Japanese Iris - ACK-COUNTABLE Ackerman, W. , 1988
Back to Japanese Master Page
  • Height: 42in (107cm)
  • Season: M
  • Falls: 6
  • Description: White ground marbled and striped violet (RHS 83B); white style arms with violet margin and lip.
    A unopened bud is illustrated on the lower left portion of the page.
  • Ack-countable is not genetically stable in color and occasional blossoms will exhibit reversion to the self color, usually only in a portion of the blossom.
    Humorously referred to as "unaccountable". Divisions in which this instability manifests itself in one blossom may or may not produce additional "mutant" blossoms. Such a mutant is illustrated below.
