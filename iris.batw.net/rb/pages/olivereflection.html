- Olive Reflection Smith, Raymond, 1990
Back to TB Master Page
  • Height: 28in (71cm)
  • Season: M
  • Reblooms: August through October
  • Description: Olive Reflection is a white reblooming iris infused with subtle colors that are difficult to appreciate in a photograph. It has standards of antique white with olive-green cast and darker styles. The falls are ruffled antique white with a white beard turning light gold deep in the throat. On the underside of the falls the midrib of the petal is olive-green and the veins lateral to this midrib are also tinted with olive green.

    It is an excellent grower with a reliable rebloom in August through October,
